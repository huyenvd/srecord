1. Introduction

Welcome to S-Record! We understand that protecting your personal information is very important. Therefore, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy seriously.

2. No Personal Information Collection

S-Record does not collect, store, or process any personal information of users. All data related to video recordings during the use of the app is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to any servers.

3. No Server Storage

We do not store any user information or data on servers. All videos and related information are stored locally on your device, and you have full control over this data.

4. User Control

Users have full control over the videos and information stored on their devices. We do not interfere with users' personal data, nor do we have the ability to access or use this data.

5. Privacy Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in functionality or applicable laws. All changes will be clearly communicated on this website.
