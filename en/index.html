Outstanding

Help sellers on e-commerce platforms such as TikTok, Shopee, Lazada...

  • Order Management

    Record detailed videos for each order, including full product information such as name, quantity, and description. In addition, the video also clearly records the current status of the order, from packaging to delivery to the customer. This process helps increase accuracy and transparency, minimizing the risk of errors during the delivery process.

  • Track Returns

    The return management system allows tracking of the entire process from when a customer requests a return until the goods are received back at the warehouse. Return management becomes easier with video recording features of the shipper delivering the returned goods. Detailed tracking helps minimize errors, ensures the return process is transparent and accurate, and minimizes loss of goods during transportation or handling. This helps businesses optimize work efficiency and enhance customer experience when there is a need to return a product.

  • This not only helps the delivery process become smoother and more efficient, but also minimizes the risks of delays and lost goods, contributing to improving service quality.

Service

S Record is a software that helps sellers on e-commerce platforms such as TikTok, Shopee, Lazada... record videos to manage outgoing orders, returned goods and goods delivered to shippers easily and friendly.

Free Software
